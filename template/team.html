Three Sigma Medicine

Led by Dr. Fuhai Li, Three Sigma is a team consisting of a group of bright professors, researchers and students from various background in computer science, mathematics, chemistry and biology. We aim at developing innovative approach to provide precision medicine for future patienets.

Dr.Fuhai Li

Fuhai Li, PhD, is an assistant professor in the Department of Biomedical Informatics, Ohio State University. His primary research interests are to apply mathematical, statistical learning, and machine learning approaches on big and diverse data sets in order to solve biological and clinical challenges in treating complex diseases, (e.g. cancers).

Haoyang Wu

Haoyang is an undergraduate student at The Ohio State University. He is the leading developer of application MD-Miner and founder of this website. Currently pursuing two majors in computer science and economics from the Ohio State University, he hopes what he learns from and contributes to Three Sigma Medicine will someday make a difference to researchers, developers or patients somewhere in the world.

Elise Miller

Elise Miller is an undergraduate honors student at Northeastern University majoring in chemical engineering and expecting to graduate in 2019. On campus, she is an active member of the American Institute of Chemical Engineers, the tennis team, and Women in Business. She expects to continue contributing to research as she explores her options to join labs on campus involved in pharmaceutical delivery and development.

Denethi Wijegunawardana

Denethi Wijegunawardana is a full time junior student at the College of Arts and Sciences in OSU. With the definitive goal of becoming an outstanding medical researcher, she is currently majoring in Microbiology with pre-Medicine.
Her main research interest is cancer cell biology and the molecular mechanism underlying the development of drugs for cancers.
